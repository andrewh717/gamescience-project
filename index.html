Instructions

    In this game your goal is to guide the blue civilian agents toward the green safety zones, while keeping them away from the red hostile agents. If an enemy comes into contact with a civilian, it's game over.

    Controls

    • W - places UP sign
    • A - places LEFT sign
    • S - places DOWN sign
    • D - places RIGHT sign
    • Left click - places DISTRACTION
    • Right click - places ALARM
